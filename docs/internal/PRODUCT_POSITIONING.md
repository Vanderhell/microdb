# Product Positioning

## One-Sentence Identity
loxdb is a deterministic durable storage core for MCU and embedded systems, designed for power-fail-safe KV/TS/REL persistence with controlled latency.

## What We Sell
- predictable embedded persistence behavior
- power-fail recovery correctness
- compact integration surface for firmware teams

## What We Do Not Sell
- general SQL database replacement
- dynamic query engine
- enterprise-scale analytics storage
